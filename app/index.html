IT Outstaffing With us

Collecting Requirements

IT outstaffing journey with Slice starts with gathering all the necessary information about your project. This procedure is free and includes gathering both project specifications and candidate requirements to ensure a perfect match.

Selecting the Team

The next step is to form a pool of candidates that meet your project requirements and are currently available for work. You will be able to look through all the pre-selected candidates' CVs and choose those who suit you in the best way.

Interviewing

After narrowing the number of candidates based on their CVs, we invite you to participate in the interviewing process. You are welcome to ask any questions as well as brief the candidates about the project details.

Recruiting

After confirming the hired specialists, we discuss the terms and conditions and prepare a standard work contract between you and Slice company.

Starting the Project 🚀

After signing the contract, you are free to kick-of the project. Slice team is also dedicated to providing you with constant support on every stage of your project till its completion.

Frequent ask qustions

What is IT Outstaffing in simple words?
Outsourcing takes place when the project is developed by a hired third-party team from A to Z. With this cooperation model the client rarely communicate with the development team and has limited control over the process. If choosing outstaffing, the hired specialists become involved in the client company's in-house processes, allowing the client to participate in the project development at all stages.
